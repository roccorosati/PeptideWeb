About PeptideMed

We exist to make cutting-edge peptide therapies accessible, affordable, and — above all — safe for every patient.

Our Mission

PeptideMed was founded on a simple belief: patients deserve access to the same advanced peptide protocols that elite physicians and professional athletes have used for years — but through a transparent, fully legal, medically supervised process.

We are a telehealth marketing and patient coordination platform. We do not prescribe, compound, or ship medications ourselves. Instead, we partner with licensed medical providers and FDA-registered compounding pharmacies to ensure every step of your care meets the highest regulatory and clinical standards.

How Our Platform Works

We coordinate every step so you don't have to.

Our Commitment to Compliance

Prescriptions Required

No peptide is dispensed without a valid prescription from a licensed physician. We strictly prohibit the purchase of any compound without medical oversight.

HIPAA Data Protection

All patient health data is stored and transmitted in accordance with HIPAA regulations. We use end-to-end encryption and never sell your personal or medical data.

FDA-Registered Pharmacies Only

We will never partner with unregulated or overseas pharmacies. Every compound dispensed through our platform comes from a domestic, FDA-registered facility.

Transparent Pricing

We clearly disclose all consultation fees, medication costs, and shipping charges before you commit to any treatment. No hidden fees, ever.
