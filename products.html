Weight Management

GLP-1 Agonist

Semaglutide

Starting at $299/mo

Semaglutide is a glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) receptor agonist that mimics gut hormones to regulate appetite and blood sugar. Clinical trials (STEP program) demonstrated an average weight reduction of 15–17% over 68 weeks.

  • Clinically proven weight loss
  • Appetite suppression
  • Improved insulin sensitivity
  • Cardiovascular risk reduction
Available as subcutaneous injection. Dosing is gradually titrated over 16–20 weeks.

Dual GIP/GLP-1 Agonist

Tirzepatide

Starting at $349/mo

Tirzepatide is a dual GIP and GLP-1 receptor agonist shown in the SURMOUNT trials to produce average weight loss of up to 22.5% — the highest of any approved weight-loss medication to date.

  • Superior weight reduction outcomes
  • Dual hormone mechanism
  • Improved metabolic markers
  • Reduced cardiovascular risk factors
Available as subcutaneous injection. Weekly dosing schedule.
